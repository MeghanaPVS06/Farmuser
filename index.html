How does it work?





We aim to provide benefit to both farmers and consumers by eliminating the middlemen market sharks and establishing a direct & healthy trade environment. Farmers can update the portal by specifying their location, quantity of vegetables available and their feasibility for payment modes. Accordingly buyer/user searching to purchase their requirements at an affordable price, can get the filtered details of the nearby areas along with the availability of the items.
